1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy covers all data processing activities carried out by GameVerse. It outlines what information we collect when you use our free online games (such as Chess, Wooden Block Puzzle, Tic Tac Toe, and others), how we use and protect that information, and your rights concerning your data. This policy does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications that may be linked from our Service; we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

2. Information We Collect and Why

To provide and continually enhance your gaming experience on GameVerse, we collect certain types of information. Our approach is to collect only what is necessary for the functioning and improvement of our services and for displaying advertisements that support our platform.

a) Information You Voluntarily Provide

GameVerse is designed for immediate play without mandatory registration. The primary instances where we might collect personally identifiable information (PII) directly from you are:

  • Direct Communications: If you contact us via email (e.g., for support, feedback, inquiries, or bug reports), we will collect your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide in your correspondence. This information is used solely to address your query, provide assistance, or act upon your feedback.

b) Information Collected Automatically (Usage Data & Analytics)

As you navigate and interact with GameVerse, our systems (and those of our trusted third-party service providers like Vercel for hosting and potentially analytics providers) automatically log certain information:

  • Device and Connection Information: This includes your IP address (which may infer your general geographic location, but is often anonymized or truncated by our analytics tools), browser type and version (e.g., Chrome, Firefox), operating system (e.g., Windows, Android), device model (less common for web), screen resolution, and language settings.
  • Interaction Data: We log how you use our Service, such as the games you play, pages visited (e.g., `index.html`, `chess_game_page.html`), duration of visit, time spent on specific pages or games, features used within games (e.g., selected difficulty, themes in Tic Tac Toe), game settings (like sound volume), high scores (for games like Wooden Block Puzzle, which are stored locally in your browser), referral URLs (how you arrived at our site), and general clickstream data. We also collect information about errors or crashes to help us improve stability.

This Usage Data is crucial for:

  • Understanding user engagement, identifying popular games and features, and pinpointing areas for improvement.
  • Optimizing game performance, user interface (UI), and user experience (UX) across different devices and browsers.
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues, bugs, and compatibility problems.
  • Informing decisions about new game development and feature enhancements based on aggregated user behavior.
  • Monitoring the overall health, security, and integrity of our Service.

c) Cookies, Local Storage, and Similar Technologies

GameVerse utilizes cookies, local browser storage, and other similar technologies (like pixels or web beacons, primarily through our ad partners) for several key functions:

  • Essential Operations & Preferences: Some cookies and local storage items are vital for the basic functioning of our games and remembering your preferences. For example:
    • Storing your chosen sound volume for game audio.
    • Remembering your selected theme in games like Tic Tac Toe.
    • Keeping track of your high scores in games like Wood Block Puzzle (stored locally).
    • Maintaining session information for smooth navigation (less common for our simple games but possible).
    These are typically session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies/local storage items that remember your settings for future visits.
  • Performance and Analytics: We may use tools like Google Analytics (if implemented in the future, this policy will be updated) or Vercel Analytics (our hosting provider offers this) which use cookies to gather aggregated, anonymous data on how users interact with GameVerse. This helps us understand traffic patterns, page load times, and user flow, all contributing to service improvement.
  • Advertising (Google AdSense): Our primary advertising partner, Google AdSense, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve personalized and non-personalized ads. These cookies help tailor advertisements based on your visits to GameVerse and other sites on the internet, making them more relevant to you. They also help measure ad effectiveness and prevent ad fraud.

Local Storage for Game Data: Please note that game-specific data like high scores (e.g., in Wood Block Puzzle for each difficulty) and user preferences (e.g., Tic Tac Toe themes, sound settings, Chess AI difficulty) are typically stored directly in your web browser's local storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers and resides on your device. You have control over this data and can clear it by managing your browser's cache and site data settings.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences: Most modern web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View active cookies and local storage items for a site.
  • Allow all cookies or block specific types or third-party cookies.
  • Delete existing cookies and local storage data.
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is issued or data is stored.
  • Utilize private browsing modes which often limit cookie persistence.
Please be aware that disabling essential cookies or local storage may impact the functionality of GameVerse (e.g., your preferences might not be saved). For detailed information on managing cookies, consult your browser's help section or visit reputable sites like www.allaboutcookies.org.

3. How We Utilize Your Information

The information we collect serves the following primary purposes, always with the goal of providing a better, more enjoyable, and sustainable free gaming platform:

  • Service Provision and Maintenance: To deliver the games (Chess, Wooden Block Puzzle, Tic Tac Toe, etc.) and features you expect, ensure stability across different browsers and devices, and maintain the operational integrity of GameVerse.
  • Personalization and Improvement: To understand user behavior and preferences, enabling us to enhance existing games (e.g., refining AI difficulty in Chess or Tic Tac Toe, adding new piece types in Wood Block Puzzle), develop new games, and tailor the user experience (e.g., remembering your last played theme or sound settings).
  • Analytics and Performance Monitoring: To track usage metrics (like most played games, session duration), identify popular content, diagnose technical problems (like slow loading times or game errors), and optimize resource allocation for our hosting and development efforts.
  • Advertising: To display advertisements via Google AdSense, which is the primary way we fund the development and maintenance of GameVerse, allowing us to offer our games for free. This may include personalized ads based on your browsing history unless you opt-out (see Section 4).
  • Communication: Solely to respond to your direct inquiries, provide customer support if you contact us, and gather feedback you voluntarily offer. We do not currently engage in unsolicited marketing communications.
  • Security and Compliance: To protect GameVerse and our users from fraudulent or malicious activity, ensure adherence to our terms (if any), and to comply with applicable legal obligations or lawful requests from authorities.

4. Advertising and Google AdSense

GameVerse is supported by advertising revenue, primarily through Google AdSense. This allows us to offer our games free of charge. Google and its partners use cookies to personalize the ads you see and measure their effectiveness.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies (such as the DoubleClick DART cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to GameVerse and/or other sites on the Internet. This is known as interest-based advertising.
  • You have control over personalized advertising from Google. You can opt out by visiting Google's Ad Settings page.
  • To opt out of other third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising, you can visit resources such as www.aboutads.info/choices (for users in the USA) or www.youronlinechoices.eu/ (for users in the EU). The Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) and the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) also provide opt-out tools.

We encourage you to review How Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services for a better understanding of their data practices and your choices.

Please note that even if you opt out of personalized advertising, you may still see ads, but they will likely be less relevant to your interests.

9. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR & CCPA Considerations)

Depending on your geographical location and applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR for individuals in the European Economic Area or CCPA for California residents), you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These can include:

  • The right to access: You may have the right to request copies of the personal information we hold about you (primarily applicable if you've contacted us directly, as most other data is Usage Data or stored locally by you).
  • The right to rectification: You may have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure (right to be forgotten): You may have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You may have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You may have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions (e.g., for direct marketing, which we don't currently do).
  • The right to data portability: You may have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
  • The right to withdraw consent: If we are relying on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawing your consent will not affect the lawfulness of any processing we conducted prior to your withdrawal, nor will it affect processing of your personal information conducted in reliance on lawful processing grounds other than consent.

To exercise these rights, or if you have questions about them, please contact us at timelytrove.9354@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

For requests related to advertising cookies and personalization, please use the opt-out mechanisms described in Section 4 (Advertising and Google AdSense).

For users in California (CCPA): The California Consumer Privacy Act provides California residents with specific rights regarding their personal information. We do not "sell" personal information in the common, monetary-exchange sense of the word. However, the CCPA has a broad definition of "sale" that may include the sharing of information for advertising purposes through cookies. As described in Section 4, you can opt-out of such "sales" (i.e., interest-based advertising) via the provided links.
