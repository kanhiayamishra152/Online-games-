A Few Things to Note:

Response Time: We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. Please allow 1-3 business days for a response, especially during busy periods.

Bug Reports: If you're reporting a bug, please be as specific as possible! Include details like:

  • Which game you were playing (e.g., Chess, Wood Block Puzzle, Tic Tac Toe).
  • What device and browser you were using.
  • Steps to reproduce the bug, if you know them.
  • Any error messages you saw. Screenshots can also be very helpful!

Game Suggestions: We love hearing your ideas for new games or features for existing ones! While we can't promise to implement every suggestion, we value your input greatly.
