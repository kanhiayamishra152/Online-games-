Important Information Before Contacting Us:

To help us assist you more efficiently, please consider the following:

  • Response Timeframe: We are a small, dedicated team (primarily Kanhaiya Mishra) and strive to respond to all legitimate inquiries as promptly as possible. Typically, you can expect a response within 1-3 business days. During periods of high volume or holidays, it might take a little longer. We appreciate your patience.
  • Reporting Technical Issues or Bugs: If you're encountering a technical problem or wish to report a bug in one of our games (Chess, Wooden Block Puzzle, Tic Tac Toe, etc.), providing detailed information is incredibly helpful. Please try to include:
    • The specific game you were playing.
    • A clear description of the issue you experienced.
    • The device (e.g., Desktop PC, Android Phone, iPhone) and web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) along with its version, if known.
    • The steps you took that led to the bug or issue. If you can reliably reproduce it, please outline those steps.
    • Any error messages that were displayed on the screen.
    • Screenshots or short video recordings of the issue can be invaluable for diagnosis. You can mention in your email if you have these available.
  • Game Suggestions and Feedback: We are always excited to hear your ideas for new games you'd like to see on GameVerse or suggestions for improving our existing titles! While we cannot guarantee that every suggestion will be implemented, we carefully consider all player feedback as it helps shape the future of GameVerse. Your creativity and insights are highly valued.
  • Partnership or Collaboration Inquiries: If you are interested in potential partnerships, collaborations, or business-related discussions, please clearly state this in your email subject line for faster routing.
  • Privacy Concerns: For any questions or requests related to your data and privacy, please refer to our Privacy Policy first. If your concerns are not addressed there, feel free to email us with "Privacy Inquiry" in the subject line.

We believe in open communication and look forward to hearing from you. Your interaction helps us build a better GameVerse for everyone!
