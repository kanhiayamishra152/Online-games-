1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Service to you. The types of information we may collect are:

Currently, GameVerse is designed to be used without requiring user registration or direct submission of personal data to play our games. However, if you choose to contact us directly (e.g., via email for support or feedback), we may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address.

b) Information We Collect Automatically (Usage Data)

When you access and use GameVerse, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our Service. This "Usage Data" may include:

  • Device Information: Your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers (like IDFA or Android Advertising ID if applicable through ad networks).
  • Log and Usage Information: Details about your visit, such as the pages of our Service you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, game progress (e.g., high scores in Wooden Block Puzzle, theme preferences in Tic Tac Toe, which are stored locally in your browser), and other diagnostic data. c) Cookies and Similar Tracking Technologies

We and our third-party partners (like Google AdSense) use cookies and similar tracking technologies (e.g., web beacons, pixels) to track activity on our Service, store certain information, and serve personalized advertisements.

What are cookies? Cookies are small text files sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. They serve various functions, like remembering your preferences, helping us analyze how our Service is used, and enabling ad delivery.

Types of cookies we may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Service to function (e.g., remembering game state or preferences like sound/theme settings).
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use GameVerse, which pages are popular, and if they encounter errors. This data is often aggregated and anonymous. (e.g., Google Analytics, if implemented).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by us and third-party advertising partners like Google AdSense to display advertisements that may be relevant to your interests. These cookies track your browsing habits across websites.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies: Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our Service may not function properly. For more information on how to manage cookies, please refer to your browser's help documentation or visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
